About Us

As the creator of the Golf Course Ratings website, I aim to create a platform that would help golfers make informed decisions about where to play and to provide a community for golfers to share their experiences and opinions about different courses. As an avid golfer, I understand that golfers want to know more about the courses we play, and our website provides information on each course's rating, slope, and par. I also understand the value of community in the sport of golf, and this website's user reviews and ratings allow golfers to connect with one another and share their love of the game. Overall, I believe that Golf Course Ratings serves an important function in helping golfers enhance their enjoyment of the game and I'm are proud to have created this resource for the golfing community.




Top Golf Courses in the United States


  1. Pebble Beach Golf Links

    Pebble Beach Golf Course is a legendary golf course located in Pebble Beach, California, that has consistently been ranked as one of the top golf courses in the world. It is known for its stunning coastal views and challenging layout, and has hosted numerous major golf tournaments, including multiple U.S. Opens. The course features narrow fairways, small greens, and numerous hazards, including bunkers and water hazards. Golfers from around the world travel to play this iconic course and experience its beauty and challenge.

  2. Liberty National Golf Course

    Liberty National Golf Course is a premier golf destination located in Jersey City, New Jersey, overlooking the New York City skyline and the Statue of Liberty. The course was designed by Tom Kite and Bob Cupp and opened in 2006. It is a links-style course with challenging and undulating greens, strategically placed bunkers, and several water hazards. The course has hosted numerous high-profile events, including the Presidents Cup in 2017, and is known for its stunning views of the Manhattan skyline. In addition to the world-class golf course, Liberty National offers a full range of amenities, including a clubhouse, restaurant, and practice facilities. It is considered one of the top golf courses in the United States and attracts golfers from around the world.

  3. The Country Club

    The Country Club is a private, members-only club that has been a fixture in Brookline, Massachusetts since its founding in 1882. The club boasts two championship golf courses, designed by Donald Ross and William S. Flynn, respectively, as well as a full range of amenities, including tennis courts, swimming pools, and dining facilities. The club has a rich history, having hosted several major golf tournaments, including the U.S. Open in 1913, the first time the event was held at a course outside of the United Kingdom. The Country Club is considered one of the most prestigious clubs in the United States, with a membership that includes many influential individuals in business, politics, and entertainment. Despite its exclusivity, the club has a strong commitment to giving back to the community and supports a range of charitable initiatives through its foundation.
